About Myself

Born in southern South America and very Passionate about Nature, some of its details along with Humankind's mathematical models were formally learnt during a 6-year-long Bsc+Msc Physics degree. During that time several personal and academic projects were accomplished, and 3 years of work experience were accumulated.

Taking great pride in being philosophically aligned with the European Union's core beliefs, looking forward to participating in projects at the most technologically advanced Latin country, and with a "Plan Quantique" scholarship to to study High-Performance-Computing and it's link with Quantum Computing via the Master in Parallel, Distributed and Quantum Computing at the recently created Behemoth the Paris-Saclay University, I entered the French technology sector.



Favourite topics

From the field of physics my favourite topics are statistical physics, complex systems, and fluid dynamics, and from Computer Science I find great joy in the fields of High Performance Computing, Optimization, and Machine Learning. Mathematical modelling is fun!


Openings & Future Projects - Work

As of January 2022 I am an intern C++ Developer at VENISE LISN-CNRS, and in May 2022 when my Quantum Technologies Scholarship finishes I'll be participating as a CFD Engineer intern during 4 months at a lab in Toulouse. As of September 2022 I'm committed to moving to Italy for personal issues, so I'm currently open to proposals to start during that period onwards, preferably on-site in one of the cities close to Rome, e.g. Pisa, Napoles, Rome itself.


Openings & Future Projects - Research

In general I'm open to participate in numerical simulations of market-related mathematical models, game theory, and their relationship with ML. In particular I have a few ideas about non-monotonous properties of Small World networks that could be verified with numerical simulations: the code is almost ready, sponsorship in terms of server time is required. This line is closely related to noisy fractional QA and their simplified variants, perhaps even with nonplanar metrics.


Other interests

My personal interests include practising sports such as squash, trekking, and pre-pandemics amateur Olympic-style weightlifting. I'm a quite histrionic individual so I enjoy joking around and playing music. With friends I like spending time watching movies and/or cooking, and I'm deeply passionate about animals. Culturally I enjoy very much both classical music in general and specific composers from the German, French and Italian baroque. My taste for Contemporary Academic Music still needs some nourishing!
