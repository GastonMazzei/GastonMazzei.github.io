I like research
Learning from others can be a lifelong process. If you combine old ideas with 'some little extra' you have what in Science is called "Research". This is something I will never ever give up.
Favourite topics
My favourite topics have always been 'statistical physics', 'complex systems' and 'numerical simulations', but recently 'high performance computing', 'machine learning' and 'quantum information' have become my main focus of attention. Nevertheless, I do remind myself periodically to not get carried away by hype in order to not leave aside the 'old' tools that were historically proven useful: for example common 'dumb' statistics and custom-tailored models that define differential equations.
I like both the academy and industrial applications
The world is full of un-optimized processes and I find the transfer of technology both personally satisfying and ethically mandatory.
Other interests
Sports, telling jokes, and counterpoint-rich music, e.g. German & French baroque.
