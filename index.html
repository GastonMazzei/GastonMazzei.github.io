I like research
Learning from others can be a lifelong process. If you combine old ideas with 'some little extra' you have what in Science is called "Research". This is something I will never ever give up.
Favourite topics
My current favourite topics are 'neural networks', 'graphical models', and 'memory management', but I like topics of artificial intelligence and statistical physics in general. Nevertheless, I do remind myself periodically to not get carried away by 'the A.I. hype' and to not leave aside the 'old' tools that were historically proven useful: for example common 'dumb' statistics and custom-tailored models that define numerically-solvable differential equations.
I like both the academy and industrial applications
The world is full of un-optimized processes and I find the transfer of technology both personally satisfying and ethically mandatory.
