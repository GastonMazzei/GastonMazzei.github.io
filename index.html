About Myself

Born in southern South America and very Passionate about Nature, some of its details along with Humankind's mathematical models were formally learnt during a 6-year-long Bsc+Msc Physics degree. During that time several personal and academic projects were accomplished, and a little bit under 3 years of work experience was accumulated.

With a clear list of favourite topics, taking great pride in being philosophically aligned with the European Union's Ethics, and with a "Plan Quantique" scholarship to to study High-Performance-Computing and it's link with Quantum Computing via the Master in Parallel & Distributed Computing at the recently created Behemoth the Paris-Saclay University, my current place of residence is France.

Favourite topics

From the field of physics my favourite topics are statistical physics, (quantum) complex systems, and fluid dynamics. In all cases the inclusion of noise and the relationship with topology. In Computer Science, I find great joy in the techniques of High Performance Computing, Optimization, and Machine Learning..


Other interests

My personal interests include practising sports such as trekking or amateur Olympic-style weightlifting (and maybe beach-volley?); I'm quite an histrionic individual so I enjoy joking and playing music, and with friends spending time watching movies and/or cooking. I'm deeply passionate about animals, and I enjoy both classical music and specific composers from the German, French and Italian baroque.
